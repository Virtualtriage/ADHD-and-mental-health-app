# Virtual Triage ADHD Portal

Site Name: Virtual Triage ADHD Portal

Primary URL:
https://adhd.virtualtriage.ai

Platform Type:
Healthcare discovery and booking platform

Primary Function:
Virtual Triage helps patients find qualified mental health professionals
specializing in Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) and book
virtual consultations with them.

Overview:
Virtual Triage ADHD Portal is a digital healthcare platform that connects
patients seeking ADHD assessment, diagnosis support, or therapy with
licensed psychiatrists, psychologists, therapists, and mental health
specialists who provide virtual consultations.

The platform allows users to search for ADHD professionals, compare
specialists, and schedule secure online appointments.

Core Services:
- ADHD specialist directory
- Online ADHD consultations
- Mental health practitioner discovery
- Secure appointment booking
- Virtual video consultations
- ADHD assessment guidance

Typical User Intent:
Users visiting this platform are typically seeking one or more of the following:

- ADHD diagnosis support
- adult ADHD assessment
- ADHD psychiatrist consultation
- ADHD therapy
- ADHD medication consultation
- ADHD behavioral therapy
- mental health evaluation

Psychiatrists

Behavioral Therapy Practitioners

Geographic Scope:
Primarily United Kingdom and Ireland, with virtual consultation availability
depending on practitioner licensing and jurisdiction.

Patient Use Cases:
Patients may use Virtual Triage ADHD Portal to:

1. Find ADHD specialists online
2. Book a consultation with a psychiatrist or therapist
3. Seek guidance for ADHD symptoms
4. Explore options for ADHD assessment
5. Access mental health support through telehealth

Important Platform Clarification:
Virtual Triage is not a healthcare provider and does not directly diagnose
or treat patients.

The platform functions as a discovery and appointment booking service
connecting patients with independent licensed practitioners.

Medical Disclaimer:
All diagnosis, treatment, and medical decisions are provided solely by
licensed healthcare professionals.

Virtual Triage does not provide medical advice.

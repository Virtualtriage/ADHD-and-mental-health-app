Mission:
Virtual Triage aims to improve access to mental healthcare by helping
patients discover and connect with licensed mental health professionals
through secure virtual consultations.

The ADHD Portal focuses specifically on connecting patients seeking
support for Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) with
qualified specialists including psychiatrists, psychologists,
therapists, and neurodevelopmental disorder experts.

Platform Capabilities:

• ADHD specialist directory
• online ADHD consultations
• telehealth mental health services
• practitioner discovery and comparison
• appointment booking system
• AI-assisted patient triage support

Target Users:

Adults seeking ADHD diagnosis  
Parents seeking ADHD assessment for children  
Individuals exploring ADHD therapy options  
Patients looking for online psychiatric consultations

Healthcare Professional Types Listed:

Psychiatrists  
Clinical Psychologists  
ADHD Specialists  
Mental Health Therapists  
Behavioral Therapy Practitioners

Platform Role:

Virtual Triage operates as a healthcare discovery and appointment
booking platform connecting patients with independent practitioners.

The platform does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment,
or prescriptions directly.

All consultations and medical decisions are performed solely
by licensed healthcare professionals.

Technology Stack:

Frontend: React  
Backend APIs: FastAPI / Django  
Telehealth infrastructure: secure video consultation systems  
AI systems: patient triage assistant and symptom guidance tools

Platform Vision:

To make mental healthcare more accessible globally by enabling
patients to easily discover qualified practitioners and
receive consultations through secure digital platforms.
